The Harvard Art Museums

Harvard University has one of the most extensive museum collections in the United States, and the Harvard Art Museums project has been preparing pieces from their Fogg, Busch-Reisinger, and Arthur M. Sackler Museums for online display and research. This project includes an important cultures list that U-Seum aims to apply in future development.

Rijksmuseum, The Netherlands

Situated in Amsterdam, the Rijksmuseum is one of Europe's foremost art museums. It houses over 8,000 selected works that span eight centuries of Dutch history and culture. Recalling the rich history of Dutch trade with East, the Rijksmuseum also features a diverse collection of arts from across Asia.
